Jane Eyre Flashcards

Fullstack app that tests users on vocabulary words from the book Jane Eyre using a spaced-repetition algorithm. Users have their progress saved and can advance to different page sets and chapter levels until the book is complete.

This project was completed over four days with my partner Nick Coleman using scrum principles and pair programming.

Frontend: React, Redux, Router, Thunk, Bootstrap

Backend: Node.js, Express, MongoDb, Mongoose, Authentication via Passport Google Oauth2

Testing: Mocha, Chai
